Bonk.io  Author : Chaz  -   10 114 918 plays

Play online and compete against hundreds of players in Bonk.io, a multiplayer arcade game. Try to be the last survivor of each level and push your opponents out of bounds to win a round. Based on an great physical engine Bonk.io is a strategy game but also a skill game. You will control precisely your avatar to push other players out of the levels and dodge their treacherous attacks to stay alive. By pressing X you will momentarily become heavier, but less agile, allowing you to counter your opponents. By creating a free account it will be possible to save your progress online, customize your avatar, but also to use the level editor and share your creation online.
